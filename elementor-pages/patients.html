Admission Process

Our admission desk is open 24/7. For planned admissions, bring your doctor's referral, identity proof, and insurance documents.

  • OPD consultation first
  • Admission slip from doctor
  • ID & insurance card ready
  • Pre-op investigations if required
🛡️

Cashless Insurance

We are empanelled with 30+ TPA and insurance providers. Our billing desk will verify your policy and initiate cashless pre-authorization.

  • Bring your insurance card & policy
  • Pre-auth done by our team
  • No upfront payment for covered treatments
  • 30+ insurance partners
🧪

Diagnostics & Lab

In-house laboratory, radiology, and blood bank services — no need to travel for tests.

  • Blood tests & pathology
  • X-Ray & imaging
  • ECG & 2D Echo
  • Emergency point-of-care testing 24/7
🚑

Emergency Services

24/7 emergency department with trained staff, trauma bays, and direct access to ICU and operation theatre.

  • Always open, always staffed
  • Ambulance on call: +91 88883 72225
  • Rapid triage under 10 minutes
  • ALS-certified emergency team
