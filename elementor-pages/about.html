Our Journey

Committed to Excellence

Welcome to Kute Hospital, inspired by the vision of Mr. Bhausaheb Damodar Kute. His dedication to improving community health laid the foundation for our Hospital & Dr. Pradeep Kute's leadership plays a significant role in the hospital's development.

Established on June 13, 2010, Kute Hospital and Laparoscopy Centre aims to bring advanced medical care to rural areas, providing treatments typically found only in big cities. Our mission is to serve communities with compassion and excellence.

Over 14+ years, we have grown into a comprehensive multispecialty facility offering emergency care, advanced laparoscopic surgery, orthopaedics, cardiology, and much more — all under one roof in Sangamner.

Meet Our Founder

Founder & Chief Surgeon

Dr. Pradeep Kute

MS (General Surgery) · Laparoscopic Surgeon

Dr. Pradeep Kute is a highly experienced general and laparoscopic surgeon with over 14 years of practice in Sangamner. His vision has been to bridge the gap between urban medical excellence and rural healthcare access.

Under his leadership, Kute Hospital has grown into a full multispecialty centre with visiting super-specialists, advanced diagnostics, and 24/7 emergency services.

  • 500+ successful laparoscopic procedures performed
  • Expert in emergency, trauma & cancer surgery
  • Pioneer of affordable specialist healthcare in Sangamner
  • Empanelled with 30+ TPA & insurance providers
